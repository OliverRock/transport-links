# What does every thing mean?


## Naphtha

A fraction of crude oil, made from fractional distilation. Flamable hydrocarbon.
Zippo fuel. Component in plastic production.
Liquid at room temp.
Transported as liquid in pipelines, ships, river boats. Generally not by road or rail.

## Cracker (Steam cracker)
Takes in hydrocarbons/naphtha, breaks larger molecules into small ones (ethylene and propylene)
It is the sink to model because it is the exit node for raw materials in the model.
A furnace train is the actual hardware which does the hot work.

## Ethylene

C2H4. Foundational building block of plastics and chemical industry. It is polymerised into polyethylene, the most common plastic in the world. 
The end product of out model.
Gas at room temp.
Transported in pipelines or refigurated/pressurized liquid.

## Crude distillation unit (CDU)

Seperates crude into fractions by boiling point. Crude -> LPG, naphtha, kerosene, diesel, etc

## Refinery

A refinary is the whole facility, whereas the CDU is the unit
